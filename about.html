About Siyasat Logic Lab

Siyasat Logic Lab is an early-stage health-tech initiative focused on improving transparency in healthcare costs for Filipinos through simple, practical digital tools.
We design systems that translate complex healthcare policies into clear, actionable information, especially for underserved and low-connectivity communities.

Our Mission

To make healthcare costs understandable before patients make critical decisions, reducing uncertainty, financial stress, and misinformation.

Overview

Problem

Many patients enter hospitals without knowing what they will pay. This lack of cost transparency leads to confusion, delayed decisions, and unnecessary financial burden.

Solution

We build lightweight, offline-capable tools that estimate hospital bills using PhilHealth case rates and user inputs, providing immediate clarity even in low-connectivity environments.

Current Product

PhilHealth Estimator (MVP) A simple calculator that predicts PhilHealth coverage and expected patient balance based on real case rates.

Vision

To become a standardized healthcare cost intelligence platform used by patients, providers, and institutions across the Philippines.

Core Capabilities

Offline-First Systems

Applications designed to function even without internet connection, ensuring usability in rural areas, hospital basements, and low-signal environments.

Healthcare Cost Logic

Structured computation of PhilHealth case rates and patient bill estimates based on real policies and user inputs.

Lightweight Web Apps

Installable browser-based tools that eliminate the need for app store downloads, reducing friction for users.

Data Structuring

Converting fragmented public healthcare data into simple, usable, and searchable formats for everyday use.

Support & Collaboration

We are currently building and refining early-stage solutions. We welcome collaboration with developers, healthcare professionals, and partners who share the goal of improving healthcare accessibility.
